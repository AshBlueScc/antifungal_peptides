Predict Peptide

Description

Antifungal peptide predictor is a server for the prediction of antifungal peptide based on a novel N-to-1 neural network ranks peptide(s) by the predicted probability that the peptide will be antifungal peptide or not. Antifungal peptide predictor aims to determine for a set of peptides how likely each of them is antifungal peptide or not. The peptides can simply be entered into the text box, and will be ranked according to their antifungal peptide prediction score. The score is not very strictly related to the probability, but if the score is higher, that the probability of the peptide is an antifungal peptide is higher too.

Input

Peptide sequences(each peptide sequence on a separate line)

Output

A ranked list of the input peptides, scored by antifungal peptide predictor. The score, between 0 and 1, is a prediction of the degree of antifungal ability. A score above 0.5 suggests that the peptide is antifungal peptide. Scores closer to 1 indicate that antifungal peptide predictor is more confident that the peptide will be antifungal peptide and scores closer to 0 suggests that the peptide is very unlikely to be antifungal peptide.
