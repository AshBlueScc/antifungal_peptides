Descreption

Antifungal peptide prediction is a server for the prediction of Antifungal peptide based on a novel N-to-1 neural network ranks peptide(s) by the predicted probability that the peptide will be bioactive.Antifungal peptide prediction aims to determine for a set of peptides how likely each of them is bioactive to be Antifungal peptide. The peptides can simply be entered into the text box, and will be ranked according to their Antifungal peptide prediction score.

Input

Peptides (up to 64kbytes)(1-letter code, each peptide on a separate line)

Output

A ranked list of the input peptides, scored by Antifungal peptide prediction. The score, between 0 and 1, is not a prediction of the degree of antifungal ability but a prediction of how likely the peptide is to be bioactive. A score above 0.5 suggests that the peptide is bioactive. Scores closer to 1 indicate that Antifungal peptide prediction is more confident that the peptide will be bioactive and scores closer to 0 suggests that the peptide is very unlikely to be bioactiove.
